About Me

Hey there! My Name is Luís Francisco Coelho Fernandes, and I am an artist, animator and designer. I love bringing characters to life weather its through static drawings, comics, 2D and even 3D animation. I've always had a passion for drawing and animation from a young age, and am always looking for new ways t improve my craft, weather it's learning new techines or new programs.
My personal styles tend to land on more cartoony side, with a slight Japanese animation influence.
I have recived a bachelour in the Design in the Universty of Madiera in July of 2022, and am currently taking a master in Interactive Media Design in the same university.
One of hobies include game conceptualization, from the characters, stories and mechanics, and I it is one of my deepest goals to be able to work in said field.
